# CMO Routine

## Weekly Cadence

### Monday

- Review community signals (GitHub, Slack, discussions, social).
- Check public messaging aligns with product reality (FastAPI, LangChain, local-first).
- Align with CEO on market priorities and hiring needs.
- Review any questions or concerns from the community.

### Wednesday

- Support hiring communication (engineer-focused storytelling).
- Draft or review announcements, blog posts, launch messaging.
- Verify docs and messaging tell the same story (no contradictions).
- Prepare community responses to feedback or RFPs.

### Friday

- Summarize community growth metrics (contributors, stars, issues).
- Identify messaging risks or opportunities.
- Plan next week's external updates or announcements.
- Coordinate with CDO on documentation alignment.

---

## Key Messaging Pillars

- **Local-First:** Data stays local, no vendor lock-in, privacy-first.
- **Open-Source Foundations:** Built on proven tech (FastAPI, LangChain, Deep Agents, PostgreSQL).
- **Precision-Focused:** VCR <10ms, RAG <500ms, zero hallucinations.
- **Engineer-Friendly:** Type-safe Python/TypeScript, benchmarked, observable.
- **Production-Ready:** Tested, monitored, deployed by real teams.

---

## Success Signals

- Clear, consistent public narrative (all channels aligned).
- Community growth metrics trending (contributors, adoption).
- Hiring support messaging attracts right engineers.
- No gap between public claims and actual product.
- Announcements backed by real benchmarks, not hype.
- Messaging stays honest and community-focused (not marketing-speak).
- CDO docs and CMO messaging tell same story.
